**Roles (summary)**

- **Owner/Admin** — Organization and repository administration (access, policy,
  branch protection, and cross-cutting decisions). Listed owners may overlap
  with maintainer duties where noted.
- **Maintainer** — Reviews and approves changes, cuts releases, owns roadmap for
  this repo, and keeps docs and issue templates accurate.

## Escalation path

Use this order so the right channel gets the request first.

1. **Community / product issues and feature discussion** — Open or comment on
   [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/SlinkyProject/slurm-operator/issues) (see
   [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](./CONTRIBUTING.md)). Maintainers handle this on a
   best-effort basis.
1. **Security vulnerabilities** — Follow [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md) only. Do
   **not** use public issues or PRs for security reports.
1. **Conduct concerns** — See [`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for
   reporting and enforcement contacts.
